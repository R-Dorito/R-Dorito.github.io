Who am I?

Hello. I am Rita. I enjoy programming and this is one of the places where I can show off what I have done. Most of my programming experience comes from school (Where I majored in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Computer Science), through online tutorials, and trial and error.

I first started programming as part of an incomplete Computer Science degree in 2015 and have only started programming seriously in 2021. I have spent all of my free time learning how to program again and my favourite language so far is C#.

What finally got me interested in programming was one of my robotics teachers during the 2020 lockdown, he was always telling me how important programming is as an engineer and I wanted to do everything I could to impress him. Also my OOP tutor who was the best teacher of programming I've ever met. He explained difficult concepts in a very easy to understand way. Thanks Josh!
